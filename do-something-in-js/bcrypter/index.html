About BCrypt
What is BCrypt?

BCrypt is a password hashing function designed to be slow and computationally expensive, making it resistant to brute-force attacks.

Salt Rounds

Higher salt rounds increase security but take longer to compute. 10-12 rounds are typically recommended for most applications.
